ADP Vantage HCM is a strategically focused, globally scalable HR system that delivers unmatched expertise and service, comprehensive unified services, innovative and intuitive technology, and valuable insights to help unlock your workforce’s maximum potential.

ADP Vantage HCM delivers innovative technology combined with actionable “business intelligence” to help to make better business decisions. It provides A single view of critical analytics across the organization for actionable, real-time decision-making, easy-to-use dashboards that let to customize views and parse data in ways that are most meaningful to specific role and organization, out-of-the-box metrics that can be sliced and diced in multiple ways.

ADP Vantage HCM helps streamline and simplify day-to-day HR activities so as to increase productivity and focus on what matters most to the core business. Provides A single HR system of record to manage, track and analyze anything and everything HR-related across your organization, configurable workflows to define and automate your unique processes, through all individuals involved, to enhance collaboration and help maximize productivity across the organization, designed to help you with your legal compliance obligations, from payroll to Health Care Reform, an interactive org chart that provides maximum insight into your employees with an actionable tool to manage all aspects of your people strategy more efficiently and effectively, ADP Mobile Solutions app allows employees to securely connect with your organization anytime, anywhere from their smartphones. They can receive company news, view upcoming events, and access a directory to call, email and locate colleagues.

ADP Vantage HCM helps improve benefits administration with a streamlined user experience and improved carrier and employer communications to increase participation. Employees can easily compare and choose the best available provider for them, complete annual enrollment, and manage other benefit-related tasks as life events change throughout the year.

The features include decision support tools educate and empower employees to make better, faster decisions about the coverage that is best for them and their family based on criteria such as deductibles and cost. Mobile access through the ADP Mobile Solutions app allows employees to view benefits plan information, track FSA balances and transactions, and access retirement plans from their smartphones, a simplified enrollment process helps minimize confusion and increase adoption with user-friendly tools and an intuitive experience, insights from clear analytics let you immediately see what is working and what needs modification so you can accomplish your benefit goals with a real strategy and plan, automated solutions help maintain compliance with changing Health Care Reform requirements and other benefits-related laws, rules and regulations.
